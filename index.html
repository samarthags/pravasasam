Sample Itinerary · ಮಾದರಿ ದಿನಚರಿ

Bachelors Coastal Road Trip

Gokarna – Murudeshwar – Udupi · 3 Days / 2 Nights

D1
Gokarna — Beach Trek & Café Hangout
ಗೋಕರ್ಣ — ಬೀಚ್ ಟ್ರೆಕ್ಕಿಂಗ್ ಮತ್ತು ಕೆಫೆ ಹ್ಯಾಂಗ್‌ಔಟ್
Check-in at beach hut/hostel. Visit Om Beach & Kudle Beach — beach trekking between beaches. Evening at 'Namaste Café' for sunset + music. Night: Beachside bonfire (Campfire).
D2
Murudeshwar Scuba + Honavar Mangrove Boating
ಮುರುಡೇಶ್ವರ ಸ್ಕೂಬಾ + ಹೊನ್ನಾವರ ಬೋಟಿಂಗ್
Morning: Drive to Murudeshwar (1.5 hrs). Afternoon: Scuba diving at Netrani Island — trip highlight. View giant Shiva statue. Evening: Sharavathi Backwaters Mangrove Boating — best Instagram reel spot. Night: Reach Udupi.
D3
Udupi — Water Sports & Island Life
ಉಡುಪಿ — ವಾಟರ್ ಸ್ಪೋರ್ಟ್ಸ್ ಮತ್ತು ಐಲ್ಯಾಂಡ್ ಲೈಫ್
Morning: Boat to St. Mary's Island from Malpe. Afternoon: Jet-skiing, parasailing, banana boat ride at Malpe. Evening: Kapu Lighthouse — panoramic sea view. Return to Bengaluru/Mangaluru.

Booking Policy

ಸ್ಪಷ್ಟ ನಿಯಮಗಳು — ಯಾವುದೇ ಗೊಂದಲ ಇಲ್ಲ

Advance Payment ಅಡ್ವಾನ್ಸ್ ಪಾವತಿ
50% advance required to confirm your booking. Remaining 50% to be paid on departure day. Advance payment secures your seat and vehicle booking.
Cancellation Policy ರದ್ದತಿ ನೀತಿ
7+ days before trip: 80% refund. 3–6 days before: 50% refund. Within 48 hours: No refund. Adventure activities (scuba, boating) cancelled due to weather: partial refund for that activity only.
ID Proof Required ಗುರುತಿನ ಚೀಟಿ ಕಡ್ಡಾಯ
Every traveller must carry a valid Aadhaar Card or other government-issued photo ID. Required for hotel check-in and adventure activity registration.
Conduct & Environment ನಡವಳಿಕೆ
Plastic use is strictly prohibited. Respectful behaviour in public places and temples is mandatory. Disruptive behaviour may result in removal from the group without refund.
Adventure Activities ಸಾಹಸ ಕ್ರೀಡೆ
All adventure activities are weather-dependent. We only partner with licensed, certified guides. Health conditions that prevent participation must be disclosed at booking.
